LUNO UAB is a trusted partner to companies and organizations for turn-key implementation of complex IT, Telco systems; their transformation, fine-tuning for core business performance.

LUNO's primary focus is engineering and technical operations, their optimization, OSS (Operations Support Systems) integration into corporate business processes, workflows of BSS (Business Support Systems). LUNO has expertise in sourcing of IT systems and has developed partnerships with leading providers of ERP, CRM, CAD and GIS systems.

LUNO has a team of systems/databases integration, software development experts and invests in software development (interfaces, add-ons, 'gap filling' products) supporting delivery, maintenance and development of integrated solutions targeted to particular businesses, industry. "Inventory3D" system  is LUNO's flagship product. It is a state-of-art system with implemented novel approach to inside-plant engineering and facilities maintenance. "Inventory3D" integrates SketchUp based 3D design, engineering and visualization with corporate ERP, GIS, CAD, SCADA, mobile operations in the field.

LUNO has provided systems installed for telecom carriers, tower companies, photovoltaic systems producers, organizers of expo shops.
